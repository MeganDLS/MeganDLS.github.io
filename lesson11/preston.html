Tut Tut and a Rock

Winnie and Robin out on a walk,
when suddenly they come across a great rock.
“Winnie,” says Robin how will we prevail?
“Tut, tut,” replied Winnie, “with a little effort we surely can’t fail.”
Together they reached, scaled and climbed,
And with great effort they tamed the incline.
“Do we have to go down? That’s way too much effort!” Robin Cried.
“Tut, tut”, replied Winnie, “will give it our best and let our hearts be our guide.”
Huffing and puffing they slumped to the bottom.
“We’ve made it!” they sighed.
and agreed to a long rest before going up the backside.
As soon as they sat
The heard a great “crack”
“Tut, tut,” said Winnie, “it looks like rain.”
